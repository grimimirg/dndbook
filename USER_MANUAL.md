## Getting Started

### First Login

1. Open your browser and navigate to the application URL

### Navigation

The application is divided into three main panels:

- **Middle Panel**: Posts and content feed
- **Right Panel**: Campaign tree and navigation

## Campaign Management

### Creating a New Campaign

1. Click the "Create Campaign" button in the right panel
2. Fill in the campaign details:
   - **Name**: Your campaign's title
   - **Description**: A brief overview of your campaign
3. Choose a **Character Creation Mode**:
   - **Optional**: Players can choose to create characters or not
   - **Free**: Players can create any number of characters freely
   - **Predefined**: Only the DM can create predefined characters that players can select from
4. Click "Create" to finalize

### Editing Campaign Details

1. Select your campaign from the tree
2. Click the edit icon next to the campaign description
3. Modify the name or description
4. Save your changes

### Deleting a Campaign

1. Select the campaign you want to delete
2. Look for the delete option (usually in the campaign menu)
3. Confirm the deletion
   - ⚠️ **Warning**: This will permanently delete all posts, characters, and data

## Character Management

### Character Creation Modes

#### Optional Mode
- Players can choose whether to create a character
- Suitable for campaigns where characters aren't central

#### Free Mode
- Players can create any number of characters
- Best for campaigns with multiple characters per player

#### Predefined Mode (DM Only)
- Only the DM (campaign owner) can create characters
- Players select from the predefined list
- Ideal for balanced parties and specific campaign requirements

### Creating a Character

1. Navigate to the Characters panel in the left sidebar
2. Click "Create Character"
3. Fill in the character details:
   - **Name**: Character's name (required)
   - **Race**: Character's race (required)
   - **Class**: Character's class (required)
   - **Description**: Backstory and personality (optional)
4. Upload a character portrait (optional)
5. If you're the DM in "Predefined" mode, check "Predefined Character"
6. Click "Confirm" to create

### Editing a Character

1. Click on a character in the Characters panel
2. Modify any details in the edit modal
3. Upload a new image or remove the existing one
4. Save your changes

### Assigning Characters (Predefined Mode)

1. As DM, create predefined characters
2. Players will see a selection modal
3. Players choose their character from the list
4. The character is then assigned to that player

## Creating Posts

### Basic Post Creation

1. Select your campaign from the right panel
2. Use the post creator at the top of the middle panel
3. Fill in:
   - **Title**: A descriptive title for your post
   - **Content**: The main body text of your post
4. Click "Create Post"

### Post Importance Level

Mark important posts with the importance system:
- Click the crown icons (👑) to set importance (1-10)
- Higher importance posts are highlighted
- Use this for major plot points, critical encounters, or important lore

### Hiding Posts from Players (DM Only)

As the campaign owner, you can hide posts from players:
1. Toggle the "Hide from Players" option when creating a post
2. Hidden posts show a lock icon (🔒)
3. Only the DM can see hidden posts
4. Use for DM notes, future plot hooks, or secret information

### Post Ordering

Posts can be reordered:
- Use the sort dropdown to change the display order
- Options include: by date, by importance, custom order
- Custom order allows you to arrange posts in a narrative sequence

## Image Galleries

### Adding Images to Posts

1. When creating or editing a post, click "Add Images"
2. Select one or more images from your device
3. Supported formats: PNG, JPEG, JPG, GIF, WebP
4. Maximum 10 images per post

### Managing Images

- **Reorder**: Use the ↑ and ↓ buttons to change image order
- **Remove**: Click the × button to remove an image
- **Add Descriptions**: Add text descriptions for each image
- **Preview**: Images are shown as thumbnails before posting

### Viewing Images

- Click any image in a post to view it in the lightbox
- Navigate between images with arrow keys or on-screen controls
- Close the lightbox by clicking outside the image or pressing Escape

## Comments and Collaboration

### Adding Comments

1. Click on a post to view its details
2. Scroll to the comments section
3. Type your comment in the text area
4. Click "Submit" to post your comment

### Comment Features

- **Real-time**: Comments appear instantly for all campaign members
- **Threaded**: Reply to specific comments to create discussions
- **Notifications**: Get notified when someone comments on your posts

### Post Viewed Status

The application tracks which posts you've viewed:
- New posts are highlighted
- Viewed posts are marked as read
- This helps you stay up-to-date with campaign developments

## Inviting Players

### Sending Invitations

1. Open your campaign
2. Click "Invite Users" in the campaign menu
3. Select users from the available list
4. Click "Send Invites"

### Accepting Invitations

1. You'll receive a notification when invited
2. Click the notification to view the invitation
3. Accept or decline the invitation
4. Once accepted, the campaign appears in your "Shared Campaigns" list

### Managing Campaign Members

- View all members in the Players panel (left sidebar)
- See who has accepted invitations
- Track player activity within the campaign

## Import/Export Campaigns

### Exporting a Campaign

1. Select the campaign you want to export
2. Click "Export" in the campaign menu
3. The application will generate a ZIP file containing:
   - Campaign metadata
   - All posts and comments
   - Character data
   - Images
4. Download the file to your computer

### Importing a Campaign

1. Click "Import Campaign" in the main menu
2. Select a previously exported ZIP file
3. Click "Upload and Import"
4. The campaign will be recreated with all its data

**Use Cases:**
- Backup your campaigns
- Transfer campaigns between instances
- Share campaign templates with other DMs
- Archive completed campaigns

## Tips and Best Practices

### For Dungeon Masters

1. **Use Importance Levels**: Mark critical plot points with high importance
2. **Hide Secret Information**: Use the hide feature for DM-only content
3. **Organize with Custom Order**: Arrange posts to match your narrative flow
4. **Predefine Characters**: Use predefined mode for balanced parties
5. **Regular Backups**: Export campaigns regularly to prevent data loss

### For Players

1. **Stay Updated**: Check notifications for new content
2. **Engage with Comments**: Discuss plot points and share ideas
3. **Track Character Progress**: Keep your character sheet updated
4. **View Unread Posts**: Use the viewed status to catch up on missed content

### General Tips

- **Add Images**: Visual content enhances the storytelling experience
- **Write Descriptive Titles**: Make posts easy to find and reference
- **Use Character Portraits**: Helps visualize the party
- **Regular Logins**: Stay engaged with your campaign's development

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Q: I can't see a post that should be there**
- A: The post might be hidden (DM only). Check with your DM.

**Q: I can't create a character**
- A: The campaign might be in "Predefined" mode. Wait for the DM to create characters.

**Q: Images aren't uploading**
- A: Check that images are in supported formats (PNG, JPEG, JPG, GIF, WebP) and under the size limit.

**Q: I'm not receiving notifications**
- A: Check your browser's notification settings and ensure they're enabled for this site.
